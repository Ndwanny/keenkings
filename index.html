About the Studio

Where Vision
Meets Innovation

Keenkings Media is a dynamic media production company specializing in storytelling, digital content creation, and brand development. We are dedicated to producing high-quality visual narratives and brand identities.

"Our commitment to creativity, innovation, and integrity drives us to deliver impactful content."

Established in 2016, we have evolved into a trusted partner for businesses and organizations looking to enhance their digital and media presence. Based in Lusaka, we serve clients with excellence and professionalism.

How It Works

My Creative Process

Discovery Call

We start with a conversation. I learn about your vision, goals, and the story you want to tell. No commitment required.

Creative Planning

From location scouting to mood boards, I prepare every detail to ensure your shoot runs smoothly and looks stunning.

The Shoot

On the day, I guide and direct while keeping the atmosphere relaxed and authentic. Great images come from genuine moments.
